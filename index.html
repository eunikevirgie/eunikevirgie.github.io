Section 1: The Rise and Fall of UK Oil Prices

The chart tracks how UK oil prices swung dramatically between 1960 and 1990. For over a decade, prices barely moved—then the 1970s hit, and everything changed. A global oil crisis sent prices soaring to nearly $40 a barrel by 1980 before tumbling again through the decade. By the late ’80s, the market stirred once more, reminding the world that oil never stays still for long.
